Modular Trading Solutions

About Lynx

Lynx was established in 2000 in New York. It designs and develops technology modules for the US Equity markets, providing complete trading solutions to Broker Dealers
Broker Benefits

Lynx becomes your outsourced technology and support service bureau.

We have all the expertise you require on the trading, technology and regulatory fronts to access the US Equity markets.

Lynx Partnership

Lynx is your technology partner, offering trading and regulatory expertise in the US Equity markets.

Our product experts work with your team to define your priorities and deliverables. We help you minimize your development costs, develop custom solutions for your clients, maintain your inbound and outbound connections with clients and vendors, manage regulatory reporting, billing, back office, analytics, routing, support.

We put it all in place for you, minimizing your time to market.

Control your Technology Platform

Our technology is adapting daily to the changing market conditions. While there are always new venues, new liquidity codes, new order types, new client systems, new algorithms, new vendor systems, new trading strategies, you don't have to worry about maintaining any of them.

We work with you to plan the development priorities based on your needs and provide you all the tools and access you need and you simply control the platform in the best way that serves your end customers

Optimal Order Routing

Lynx maintains connectivity and access to all venues, exchanges, dark pools, market makers.

Your orders are routed to the street in your name, we help you optimize your liquidity access and minimize your costs by managing which venues to access and when.

We work with you on maintaining all the necessary relationships with exchanges, brokers, market makers, dark pools. We will make suggestions on what venues to access directly, how to reduce costs, help optimize liquidity capture for your end clients, minimize their costs and maintain their anonymity.

Our continuous research and development cycle allows you to maintain updated routing tables that adapt to real time market conditions relative to the nature of your order flow.

Connectivity Aggregator

This module allows Broker/Dealers to receive, monitor and direct order ﬂow from multiple clients and multiple OMS/EMS platforms. The customer is able to set up simple routing logic directing order ﬂow one-to-one to downstream destinations.
Standard Package:
  • FIX Connectivity Inbound from multiple entry points
  • Connectivity Outbound to multiple exit points (FIX or exchange native)
  • Simple 1-1 routing rules (if customer is A, send to broker B)
  • Internal market data
  • Connection Setup and Status (Customer or Venue) & Flow Monitoring GUI
  • EOD or T+1 order level, trade level, predeﬁned reports
ADD-ONS:
  • Order API
  • 15c3-5
  • Short Order Marking (requires SOD Positions)
  • Summary Reports
  • Custom Reports
PRICING:
  • Connectivity costs passed back to client (e.g. cross connects, exchange port fees)
  • Nominal fee for certiﬁcation & connectivity with each platform (inbound & outbound)
